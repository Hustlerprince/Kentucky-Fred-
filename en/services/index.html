What we do

Individual software made in Switzerland

Software development

We are open to all of your ideas. Beginning from web portals, intranet solutions to online shops, automation of business logic and the migration from previous MS Excel solutions. We are also happy to help you digitize your business processes.

Freelancing

We have many years of experience as freelancers and are happy to support English and German-speaking teams with programming, code reviews and consulting. Whether remotely or on site in Switzerland, we are happy to be part of your development process.

Product development

Do you have a product idea and need a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) or complete implementation? We are also happy to support you as a classic web agency.

Technologies

We feel at home in many technologies, with an emphasis on web technologies. Naturally, HTML, JavaScript and (S) CSS are used in the front end. We like to use new languages such as Crystal or more traditional ones such as Ruby.
We are particularly happy to implement ideas with the Ruby on Rails web framework, as it enables the greatest possible productivity and speed in development.
Companies such as Shopify, Bloomberg, Zendesk, Kickstarter, CrunchBase, GitHub, Fiverr, SlideShare, Soundcloud, Couchsurfing, Groupon, Scribd, Netflix, Twitter, Hulu, World Bank and Xing.com have put their trust in this framework.
